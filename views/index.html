About

After graduating from the Connecticut School of Broadcasting back in 2015, Cory began his media career as a reporter for a local news outlet in Wilmington, North Carolina. After becoming an accomplished writer, he seized his first opportunity to jump head first into radio, his true passion, and never looked back.

Working as a creative director, voice talent, producer and news host, Cory currently represents several local radio stations, and works with clients ranging from local businesses to national agencies and companies such as Live Nation, Sears, Hardee's, Blue Bell Creameries and many more.
